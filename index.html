The Harvest

Premium Varieties

Discover the unique flavor profiles of Pakistan's most celebrated mango varieties.

Chaunsa (Chausa)

Punjab • Mid Summer

Known as the 'King of Mangoes'. Exceptionally sweet, rich in aroma, and bursting with a heavenly, juicy texture.

Sindhri

Sindh • Early Summer

Large, elongated, and highly aromatic. Features a firm, fiberless flesh that melts in your mouth with a delicate sweetness.

Anwar Ratol

Punjab • Mid Summer

Small in size but intensely sweet. Offers a unique, concentrated mango flavor and a highly fragrant aroma.

Langra

Punjab • Mid Summer

Retains a green skin even when fully ripe. Known for its distinct, slightly tangy and complex sugary flavor.

Dasehri

Sindh & Punjab • Early Summer

Elongated with a sweet, aromatic profile and incredibly smooth, fiberless flesh. A classic favorite.
